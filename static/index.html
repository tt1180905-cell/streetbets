What StreetBets Measures

StreetBets is a Black-Scholes accuracy validation lab for Indian index options. At 5 fixed times each trading day it captures the Nifty and Sensex option chain (±5% from spot, nearest expiry) and generates a prediction matrix — for every (predicted spot %, prediction day) combination it computes what each contract would theoretically be worth using BSM with strike-specific IV and calendar-day T convention. At 3:35 PM it reconciles against actual closing prices and measures how far projected PnL was from actual PnL. The goal: validate whether BSM is accurate enough to power a retail-facing PnL calculator feature on DreamStreet.
