What StreetBets Measures

StreetBets is a BSM accuracy lab for Indian options. At fixed times each trading day, it captures the full Nifty and Sensex option chain (±5% from spot, nearest expiry) and generates a prediction matrix: for every combination of (predicted spot move, prediction day), it computes what each contract would theoretically be worth using Black-Scholes pricing.


At EOD (3:35 PM IST), it reconciles predictions against actual closing prices, computing real vs projected PnL for every contract at every snapshot. The goal: validate whether BSM-derived predictions are accurate enough to surface to retail users as a PnL calculator feature.
